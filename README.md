# 🛒 ShopZY — E-commerce Sales Optimization

A business analysis project exploring how a mid-sized e-commerce platform can make smarter decisions around revenue, discounts, and regional performance — built using Power BI.

---

## 👋 About This Project

This project simulates a real-world business scenario where an e-commerce company struggles with inconsistent revenue, heavy discount dependency, and unclear product performance.

The goal is to apply a **Business Analyst mindset** to:
- Analyze sales data  
- Extract insights  
- Recommend actionable strategies  

---

## 🏢 1. Company Overview

**ShopZY** is a mid-sized e-commerce platform operating across multiple regions, offering products in categories like:
- Electronics  
- Accessories  
- Wearables  

The company follows a model similar to Amazon/Flipkart, focusing on:
- Wide product range  
- Competitive pricing  
- Fast delivery  

---

## 🎯 2. Business Problem

Despite steady order volumes, ShopZY is facing:
- Inconsistent revenue across regions  
- Heavy reliance on discounts  
- Lack of visibility into product performance  

### 📌 Problem Statement
ShopZY lacks data-driven insights into sales performance, discount effectiveness, and regional trends, leading to inefficient decision-making and missed revenue opportunities.

---

## 🎯 3. Objectives

- Analyze sales performance across regions  
- Identify top-performing products and categories  
- Evaluate impact of discounts on revenue  
- Provide actionable business recommendations

## 💡 Business Insights

### 1. Profitability Concern Despite Strong Revenue
- Revenue: ₹18.73M  
- Profit: ₹6.17M  
👉 High discounts and costs are reducing margins  

### 2. Heavy Dependency on Discounting
- Sales (₹21M) > Revenue (₹18.73M)  
👉 Growth is discount-driven, not sustainable  

### 3. High Average Order Value
- AOV: ₹37.47K  
👉 Customers are purchasing high-value products  

### 4. Regional Revenue Concentration — South
- Highest revenue (~₹5M)  
👉 Strong market performance  

### 5. Underperformance in West Region
- Lowest revenue (~₹3.8M)  
👉 Needs targeted improvement  

### 6. Lack of Product Leadership
- Products contribute evenly (~₹4–5M)  
👉 No clear hero product  

### 7. Diversified Payment Behavior
👉 Even distribution across methods (low dependency risk)  

### 8. Inconsistent Sales Trend
👉 Fluctuating monthly sales (unstable demand)  

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

### 1. Optimize Discount Strategy
- Use targeted discounts instead of blanket offers  
👉 Improves profit margins  

### 2. Strengthen South Region
- Invest more in marketing & inventory  
👉 Scalable revenue growth  

### 3. Improve West Region Performance
- Localized campaigns  
- Better logistics  
👉 Balanced regional growth  

### 4. Product Differentiation Strategy
- Focus on high-margin products  
👉 Better positioning & profitability  

### 5. Leverage High AOV
- Upselling & cross-selling  
- Product bundling  
👉 Higher revenue per customer  

### 6. Stabilize Sales
- Loyalty programs  
- Seasonal campaigns  
👉 Predictable revenue  

### 7. Promote Digital Payments
- Cashback & offers on UPI/cards  
👉 Faster transactions
